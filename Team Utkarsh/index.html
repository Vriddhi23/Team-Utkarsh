Our Story

Experience

Our journey began with the team selection process where we were tasked with identifying the most talented and dedicated students from various departments such as Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, and Mechatronics. After reviewing dozens of applications, we finally formed our team and started working on designing our robot. The designing phase was both challenging and exciting. We brainstormed and explored various ideas, created multiple designs, and finally settled on one that we felt would give us the best chance of success. We spent countless hours working on the details, ensuring that every part of the robot was carefully crafted and optimized for maximum efficiency. The first stage of the competition was nerve-wracking, to say the least. We knew we had put in a lot of hard work, but we were up against some of the best teams in the region. Despite the pressure, we remained focused and confident in our robot's abilities. We put our design to the test, and it performed remarkably well,we got AIR 6 in stage-1 earning us a place in the next stage of the competition. The second stage of the competition was even more intense. We had to face even tougher challenges that tested our robot's ability to maneuver, manipulate objects, and work in sync with other robots. We encountered some unexpected setbacks, but our team remained composed and quickly adapted to overcome them.
